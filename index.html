Projects

Clear Vue

In my Sophomore year of college, my friend and I participated in a hackathon at Wentworth Intitute of Technology. We created a website that uses Javascript to simulate climate change in the U.S. over 5 years by adjusting the percentages of automobiles, factories and farms in the country. We researched the average needs for these three categories in each state as well as average temperatures and other variables. We then created an algorithm to calculate the estimated change in air temperature in each state as a result of changing the categories. The website is interactive as you can adjust the sliders to indicate whichever values you desire.
